## [1.0.0] — 2026-06-29

### Added

- **Subscription tracking** — Add and manage unlimited bills and subscriptions with name, amount, billing cycle, category, and due date
- **Paused subscription state** — Mark subscriptions as paused to exclude them from spending totals without deleting them
- **Dashboard** — Spending overview showing monthly and annual totals across all active subscriptions
- **Smart reminders** — Configurable push notifications before subscription due dates
- **Category organization** — Assign categories to subscriptions for grouped filtering and reporting
- **Search and filter** — Quickly find subscriptions by name, category, or status
- **Mint CSV import** — Import existing subscription data directly from a Mint export file for seamless migration
- **JSON and CSV export** — Export all subscription data in open formats for portability and backup
- **Local-first storage** — All data stored on-device with no account, no sign-in, and no bank linking required
- **Google Drive backup** — Optional automatic backup to the user's personal Google Drive (Premium)
- **Price history tracking** — Log and view price changes over time for any subscription (Premium)
- **Ad-free experience** — Remove all ads with a Premium subscription (Free tier is monetized via AdMob)
- **Premium subscription** — Unlock Premium features for $3.99/month via Google Play Billing
